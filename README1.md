# First-step-in-SATD-with-Maldonado
https://www.researchgate.net/publication/312517371_Using_Natural_Language_Processing_to_Automatically_Detect_Self-Admitted_Technical_Debt
This is mine implement for the research paper "Using Natural Language Processing to Automatically Detect Self-Admitted Technical Debt" 2017

## Contribution
The main contribution of this paper is the maldonado dataset which is widely used in the subject of identify SATD.
They've also built a Logistic Regression model (to be specific they call it a maximum entropy classification model in the paper)
to identify comment that indicate SATD

## Personal Experience
Eventhough the propose model is rather simple and as mention before, the biggest contribution is their dataset. It's still a good
paper if you're new to SATD or TD in general. They have good explanation of SATD type definition and their model resulting quite a commendable
output - keyword that show SATD.
